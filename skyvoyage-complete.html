How do I correct a name spelling?

Minor spelling corrections of up to 3 characters can be done via our Support Desk. Major name changes require flight cancellation and rebooking.

What is the refund processing time?

Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation success. Credits will be sent back to the original payment method.
